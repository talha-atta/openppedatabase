Welcome to the Open Source PPE Database.

The Open Source PPE Database is a open data base of suppliers and manufacturers of Personal Protective Equipment (PPE).

View Database

Why this exists?

COVID-19 has disrupted supply chains for Personal Protective Equipment (PPE). PPEs are crucial to maintaining safety of people as quarantine is lifted. PPE shortages increase the risk of the spread of infection. I haven't been able to find a complete list of PPE suppliers to buy from, so here's a first attempt to put all of them together.

Free & Open Source

There are absolutely no fees. No listing, transaction, or other fees.

Focused only on PPE

This database focuses on PPE only, and will stay that way.

Free Supplier Access

Gives an easy one-stop access to both established and new PPE suppliers.
